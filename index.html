Knowing the temples

Mexico Temple



The temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints are built for the purpose of providing ordinances and ceremonies considered sacred to its members and necessary for individual salvation and family exaltation.

The architect of the temple was Emil B. Fetzer designed the building based on a book on Mayan architecture given by Heber Grant Taylor and his wife 18 years earlier, coming from the library of Taylor's grandfather, Heber J. Grant.

Every year around the time of the Christmas festivities, it is customary to decorate the visitor center and temple grounds with hundreds of Christmas lights.
