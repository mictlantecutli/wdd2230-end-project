Reception

Reception Wedding

We have the wedding reception service. We have an exclusive and beautiful space to receive your guests, after the sealing. So we have different packages to make your wedding unforgettable, we take care of everything you need, without you having to worry about logistics. All you have to do is select the packages that you like the most.

Wedding Packages

Wedding Gold

Decorated with white canvas. White chairs for the ceremony. Centerpiece with national seasonal flowers Ironing and/or steaming of the couple's attire for the wedding day. A hair and makeup service at the SPA. Bouquet for the bride with seasonal national flowers. Sound System for the Ceremony.

Wedding Esmerald

This package includes decoration, wedding cake and much more. Private reception in a banquet hall for three hours with a special menu and waiter service. Centerpiece for the ceremony (roses)

Wedding Sapphire

Manicure and Pedicure with lavender essential oils for the bride. Mariachi (musicians playing Mexican music for an hour during the ceremony). Special beauty salon package for the bride (hair and makeup) accompanied by strawberries, and all of the Esmerald Package.

Wedding Diamond

Use of private location for reception for 5 hours. Plated dinner or Buffet (Terms and conditions subject to each Resort). Wedding cake. Illuminated dance floor. DJ for 5 hours (includes sound system and lighting).

Wedding Ruby

Bouquet of flowers for the bride and groom Wedding cake and flavored water for up to 10 guests. 15% discount on all spa treatments. Newlywed package.
